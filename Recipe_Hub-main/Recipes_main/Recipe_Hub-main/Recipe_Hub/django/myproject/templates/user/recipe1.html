Smoked salmon roll on cucumber

Main Course

Looking for a show-stopping party food idea that’s both elegant and easy to prepare? The Chatelaine smoked salmon roll on cucumber is your ultimate easy entertaining recipe. It will impress guests while keeping preparation stress-free. This delightful appetizer combines nutrition and flavor in a single bite. Smoked salmon provides an impressive nutritional profile. It offers 18-25 grams of protein per 100 grams and heart-healthy Omega-3 fatty acids. These are linked to a 30% reduction in cardiovascular disease risk.

Ingredients

  • 200g high-quality smoked salmon
  • 1 large fresh cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 100g cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to Make

  1. Slice the cucumber into thin, even slices using a knife or mandoline.
  2. Lay the cucumber slices flat on a clean surface or serving plate.
  3. Spread a small amount of cream cheese on each cucumber slice.
  4. Top each slice with a piece of smoked salmon.
  5. Sprinkle chopped fresh dill and chives over the salmon.
  6. Add a touch of lemon zest and a drop of lemon juice for extra flavor.
  7. Season lightly with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  8. Roll or fold each cucumber slice gently, securing with a toothpick if needed.
  9. Garnish with capers or microgreens if desired, and serve chilled.
